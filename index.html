About

Hey there, my name is Raymond Thurman and here is a little bit of background about me. I was born in Louisville Kentucky. I am 30 years old, I have been married 8 years to my gorgeous wife and we have two beautiful kids.

I have spent close to 10 years in the US Army and led a very successful military career. I made the rank of Staff Sergeant, deployed 4 times and loved every minute of it. I had been medically discharged in 2015 and slowly started working toward my coding career.

I originally had worked on training myself to code, but found that I lacked the structure to properly learn at a decent pace. At that point I had decided to use my GI Bill to go back to school. After 2 semesters of full time work along with going to school full time and only having taken one class even related to computer science and programming, I began looking at a few other options.

It was at that point when I had found Lambda School. Lambda School is a coding bootcamp that guarantees you a career in software engineering upon graduating. I was very skeptical at first but decided to do the pre-course work and see where it led. I was accepted to Lambda School and I start classes March 18th 2019.
